FEATURES

The Dependa Doc team strives to provide the best possible experience for all clients. Additional customization of the tools in the Dependa Doc experience can be discussed, but here are some of the features that come with the basic Dependa Doc package!

High Quality Video Conferencing

Dependa Doc uses high quality video to enhance the experience for both the medical professional and the patient. Using 1080P HD resolution, Dependa Doc makes sure that every visit to the doctor's office is as realistic as possible.

Accessible Doctor's Notes

The Dependa Doc platform makes it easy for both the doctor and the patient to access the doctor's notes during their session. By integrating a note-taking tool into the software, doctors can take notes inside the meeting, and patients can easily find notes as well.

Live Closed Captions

Here at Dependa Doc, we practice what we preach. Accessibility, in all senses of the word, is our highest priority. That's why we have integrated closed captioning in all our meeting systems. Our closed captioning system is the best in the industry, and we pride ourselves on our efficient, accurate visual presentation of verbal communication.

Medical Tools Suite

Dependa Doc also includes a variety of technological tools within its software. Medical professionals have access to an industry-standard note-taking and report-generating system that integrates directly into their local administrative system.

Patient Queue

The team here at Dependa Doc understands that healthcare providers get busy. Our patient waiting room allows your patients to join the session whenever they want and alerts you when they've joined your room. This way, medical professionals are able to see when their next patient has checked in.
