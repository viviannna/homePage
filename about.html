OUR HISTORY


2018

After Dr.Ramos became increasingly aware of the issues surrounding medical telecommunications and the struggle of finding a dependable service, she decided to make a change through creating her own company. This is how DependaDoc was born! To make this a reality, in spring of 2018, she reached out to two of her acquaintances, Edward Brown and James Robert, and the team began the planning process.


2019

Upon finalizing the various stages of work needed to plan and create the best possible version of DependaDoc, the team launched our service! We had an amazing launch, and in just the first year of providing quality medical telecommunications, DependaDoc became a popular name with over 5,000 clients across America and 10,000 worldwide!


2020 - Now

The DependaDoc team has grown as our services became more and more widespread, but at heart we still uphold the values of Dr.Ramos. Ever since our founding, we constantly strive to improve DependaDoc and maintain the highest standard quality for our clients. DependaDoc is here for you, and we will always ensure that you receive the quality telemedicine you deserve!
